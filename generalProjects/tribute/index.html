Chris Kyle

"The Legend", "Devil of Ramadi", "Tex", "American Sniper"

Info

  • 1974 - Born in Odessa, Texas
  • 1982 - Recieves his first gun
  • 1992 - Graduates High School
  • 1999 - Joins the Navy
  • 2003 - First Kills as a sniper
  • 2004 - The Battle of Fallujah
  • 2006 - The Battle of Ramadi
  • 2009 - Leaves the Navy
  • 2013 - Passed Away
